ℹ️ How to read this chart

What it shows: Each bar represents one drug. The stacked segments show the distribution of patient-reported symptom outcomes across all symptoms treated with that drug. Hover the legend swatches for outcome definitions.

Controls

Sort by
Orders the drug list by the selected metric. "Any Improvement" = mild + moderate + significant + resolved. "Significant Improvement" = significant + resolved only.
Min reports
Minimum number of symptom-outcome reports for a drug to appear. Higher = fewer drugs shown but more reliable distributions.
Show as %
Toggle between percentage view (bar width = proportion) and absolute count view.

Drill-down

Click any bar to open a panel listing individual case reports for that drug, with demographics, illness duration, and per-symptom outcomes attributed to the drug.
