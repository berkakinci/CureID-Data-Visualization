CureID Long COVID — Drug Efficacy Visualizations

Interactive visualizations of patient-reported treatment outcomes from 616 Long COVID case reports on the FDA/NCATS CureID platform.

  • Symptom × Drug Efficacy Heatmap

    Drug performance vs. baseline, by symptom. 14 symptoms × 86 drugs. Color by co-occurrence lift, improvement rate, or attributed outcomes. Click any cell for case-level detail.

  • Drug Outcomes (Patient-Reported)

    Stacked bar chart of per-drug outcomes as reported by patients. Sort by score, improvement rate, or volume. Click any bar to browse individual case reports.

Data source: CureID Long COVID (FDA/NCATS) · GitHub repo
